The Mission: to develop and promote safe, healthy, and prosperous neighborhoods for low and moderate income families.

GVNPC- Greater Ville Neighborhood Preservation Commission Â  GVNPC was organized in August of 2007 as a Missouri not for profit public charitable organization under the Code section listed as 170(b)(1)(A)(vi), and is exempt from Federal income tax under section 501(c)(3) of the Code. GVNPC services Neighborhoods of St. Louis, Missouri were once vibrant and culturally rich communities have experienced social and economic decay for many years. The GVNPC believes that the way to stop the decline and to repair the decay is to develop a series of healthy and viable residential nodes of housing and commercial development as a base for urban renewal. The Organization intends to address community deterioration by encouraging, assisting, and coordinating the efforts of individual residents and organizations which would provide affordable and structurally appealing housing for people of low to moderate incomes.
